For citizens of 97 countries, Indonesia offers a Visa On Arrival (VOA) or Electronic Visa On Arrival (EVOA), which allows entry for leisure purposes including tourism, business meetings, and government visits.

Duration: 30 days, with an option to extend for an additional 30 days (total of 60 days maximum stay).

see eligible nationalities for Visa On Arrival (VOA)

Information: Indonesia offers a variety of Single entry visitor visa, designed to accommodate different needs and activities for foreign nationals. This type of visa allow for one-time entries into Indonesia for specific purposes, such as tourism, business, investment, filming, and more.

Most C visas: Are valid for 60 days from the date of entry and can be extended twice for 60 days each, resulting in a total stay of 180 days.

Exceptions:
- Visas C12, C22A, and C22B: Initially issued for 180 days and can be extended once for another 180 days, allowing a total stay of 360 days in the country. These visas are intended for longer stays related to investments, academic internships, or company internships.
- Visas C7, C7A, C7B, C7C: initially issued for 30 days and are not eligible for extension

Indonesia offers a variety of Multiple Entry D Visas, designed to accommodate different needs and activities for foreign nationals. These visas allow multiple entries into Indonesia for specific purposes, such as tourism, business, investment, filming, and more.

Flexibility: Allows for multiple entries into Indonesia, making it ideal for frequent travelers.
Extended Validity: Depending on the type, visas are valid for 1, 2, or 5 years, reducing the need for frequent visa renewals.
Purpose-Specific: Each visa type is tailored for specific activities, ensuring that foreign nationals can engage in their intended activities without legal complications.
